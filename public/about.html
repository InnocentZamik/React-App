01 : About me

Pryme at a glance.

Pryme is a young artist, rapper and song writer, he grew up and lived in Cape Town – Kraaifontein, before moving to Upington in the Northern Cape at the beginning of 2023. Pryme is a Hip/Hop artist and his style is mostly Trap Music but he don’t like being boxed in just one genre. When he began his music career at age 13, he looked up to Lil’ Wayne “I grew up listening to his music. The amount of work he puts in his craft is very impressive and he is the person I really look up too” and still till today, Lil’ Wayne is a major influence on Prymë, even the love Prymë has for tattoos is prove that Lil’ Wayne has a huge influence on him. When Prymë work on new music, he makes sure not to listen to other artist and he listens to his own music to improve on himself as he sees himself as his own competition.

Performance

Pryme performed at events such as Café Wallacedene, Adwa Café and V3 Curve to name a few.

Interviews
